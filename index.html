Synopsis

The Poppy War, written by R.F. Kuang, is a critically acclaimed grimdark fantasy novel inspired by Chinese history, mythology, and the Second Sino-Japanese War. The story follows Fang "Rin" Runin, a determined war orphan who secures a place at the elite Sinegard Academy through sheer grit and intelligence.
As Rin uncovers her latent shamanic powers, she becomes entangled in a brutal conflict that tests her resolve, morality, and humanity. The novel is a poignant exploration of the cost of war, the consequences of power, and the resilience of the human spirit.
